Swagat

Rajputana ki shaan aur clans ki kahaniyan yahan chhupi hain. Yeh secret page ab Rajput heritage ka ek chhota sa khoj hai.

Main Clans

Rajput clans mein kuch famous hain: Rathore, Chauhan, Sisodia, Kachhwaha, Bhati, Solanki aur aur bhi. Har clan ki apni kahani hai.

Veerta ki Kahani

Rajput veeron ne apni dharti ki raksha ki, mahal banaye aur kahaniyan likhi. Yeh shaan ab bhi jeevant hai.

Rathore Clan

Marwar ke rulers, veer Rana Pratap ke vamsaj. Unhone Mughals se ladai ki aur apni azadi banayi rakhi. Jodhpur aur Bikaner ke maharajas is clan se hain.

Chauhan Clan

Ajmer aur Delhi ke kings, Prithviraj Chauhan ki kahani famous hai. Unhone Rajputana ki shaan badhai aur Mughals se bhi ladai ki.

Sisodia Clan

Mewar ke rulers, Rana Sanga aur Rana Pratap ke vamsaj. Chittorgarh ki veerta ki kahani yahan se shuru hoti hai. Udaipur ke maharanas.

Kachhwaha Clan

Jaipur ke founders, Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh ke vamsaj. Unhone science aur architecture mein yogdaan diya. Amber fort banaaya.

Bhati Clan

Jaisalmer ke rulers, Rawal Jaisal ke vamsaj. Thar desert mein apni shakti banayi rakhi. Golden fort ka naam famous hai.

Solanki Clan

Gujarat aur Rajasthan ke kings, Anhilwara ke rulers. Somnath temple ki raksha ki. Chalukya vams se judi hain.

Baghel Clan

Rewa aur other states ke rulers. Bundelkhand mein shakti. Orchha ke maharajas is clan se hain.

Tanwar Clan

Delhi aur Haryana ke kings. Tomar vams se judi. Gwalior fort banaaya. Veer Suraj Mal ki kahani famous.

Rajput History aur Princely States

Rajputana ki Shaan

Rajputs ne India ki history mein mahatvapurna bhoomika nibhai. Unhone apni veerta aur sanskriti se desh ko samarpit kiya. Mughals, British aur dusre invaders se ladai ki.

Princely States

British raj mein Rajput states jaise Jodhpur, Jaipur, Udaipur, Jaisalmer princely states the. Maharajas ne apne rajyon ko chalaaya aur culture ko badhaaya.

Famous Rajputs

Prithviraj Chauhan, Rana Pratap, Rana Sanga, Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh, Maharani Gayatri Devi — ye sab Rajput history ke heroes hain.

Culture aur Heritage

Rajput culture mein forts, palaces, festivals aur martial arts ka mahatva hai. Amber, Chittorgarh, Mehrangarh forts world-famous hain.
